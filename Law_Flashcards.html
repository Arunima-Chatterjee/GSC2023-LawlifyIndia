Constitutional Law

This category of law deals with the constitution of India, which is the supreme law of the country. It sets out the structure and powers of the government, as well as the fundamental rights and duties of citizens.

Civil Law

This category of law deals with the legal rights and obligations of individuals and organizations, such as contracts, property rights, and torts.

Criminal Law

This category of law deals with crimes and offenses committed by individuals, such as murder, theft, and fraud. It also sets out the punishment for such offenses.

Labour Law

This category of law deals with the rights and obligations of employers and employees, including minimum wages, working conditions, and industrial disputes.

Tax Law

This category of law deals with the rules and regulations related to taxation, including income tax, sales tax, and customs duties.

Corporate Law

This category of law deals with the legal framework for the formation, operation, and dissolution of corporations, including corporate governance, shareholder rights, and mergers and acquisitions.

Environment Law

This category of law deals with the protection and preservation of the natural environment, including air and water quality, waste management, and biodiversity conservation.

Intellectual Property Law

Family Law
